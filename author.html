About The Author!

Hi! My name is Erika and I am currently a student studying computer science. My goal is to go into the more creative side of computer science such as UI/UX and perhaps study further in the arts. However, if money did not exist, I would love to own a bakery and a flower shop! Some little fun facts about me, my mbti is INFP, my hogwarts house is hufflepuff, I have a strong interest in floriography (the language of flowers) and am looking to make bouquets for people based on their meanings. I also enjoy collecting things such as figurines and other knick knacks. I especially adore ones that come in mystery boxes. The thrill of not knowing what I will get brings me a lot of joy when opening them. In my down time I enjoy doing art, playing video games and sleeping! I currently have been playing Minecraft, League of Legends and Hogwarts Legacy! I also enjoy having my head up in the clouds and letting my imagination take control. Fantasy ideas such as magic or medieval works gets me very excited and I love talking and hearing about them. If you're interested in fantasy, anime and games, I hope you continue to follow my page!
